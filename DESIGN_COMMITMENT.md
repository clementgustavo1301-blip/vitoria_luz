🎨 **DESIGN COMMITMENT: Rose Care Minimalist**

- **Geometry**: Soft Rounded (24px - 32px) for a humanized, friendly medical feel.
- **Typography**: 
    - **Header**: `Playfair Display` (Serif) for authority and elegance.
    - **Body**: `Plus Jakarta Sans` (Modern Sans) for readability and clean healthcare vibe.
- **Palette**: 
    - **Care Rose**: #A58079 (Deep Muted Pink)
    - **Soft Pearl**: #F8F1EF (Background)
    - **Healing Gold**: #D4AF37 (Subtle Accents)
    - *No Purple (Purple Ban ✅)*
- **Effects/Motion**: 
    - Floating Glassmorphism for Navbar.
    - Staggered Fade-in animations for section entries.
    - Magnetic feel for the main CTA.
- **Layout uniqueness**: **Fragmented Narrative**. Instead of a standard split hero, I will use a typography-first hero with an overlapping image of Vitoria, breaking the 50/50 split habit.
